I lead experience strategy for complex, large-scale ecosystems at global enterprises — turning commercial objectives into products that serve millions of users and move real business metrics. I build high-performing teams, align Design, Engineering, Product, and Data with Legal, Risk, and Compliance around a single strategy, and solve enterprise problems where the stakes are highest.
