About Me

Strategic Design Executive

UX Strategy & Digital Transformation · Finance · Healthcare · Consumer Goods

With over 20 years of experience, I lead the digital transformation of complex, large-scale ecosystems for global enterprises — translating high-level commercial objectives into intuitive digital products that serve millions of users while driving measurable bottom-line impact.

I thrive at the intersection of design, technology, and business strategy — building and mentoring high-performing teams, aligning Design, Engineering, Risk, and Product around a unified vision, and using design thinking to solve the enterprise problems where the stakes are highest.
